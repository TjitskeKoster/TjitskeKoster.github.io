I was a guest on the Dutch podcast [All the Cyber Ladies](https://www.allthecyberladies.nl). We discussed post-quantum cryptography and the research of the [Young Talents](/projects/Young_Talents) on post-quantum cryptography.

During the podcast, we discussed how quantum computers could break existing security systems and why new encryption methods are urgently needed. I also share my experiences as a young woman navigating the worlds of cybersecurity and academia.

The episode offers listeners an accessible look at a complex technical topic, highlighting both innovation and diversity in the cyber realm.
